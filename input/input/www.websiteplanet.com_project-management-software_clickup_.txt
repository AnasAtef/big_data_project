<h3>Fair Prices for a Comprehensive Service</h3>
<p>In addition to three paid plans, ClickUp offers a generous forever free plan. <strong>The</strong><strong> free plan is a good option for individuals and small teams</strong> as it comes with 100MB of storage and access to most major features, including Chat, Docs, and time tracking.</p>
<p>That said, <strong>there are limitations to the free version, mainly in the number of times you can use certain features</strong>. For instance, you can only create five Spaces (with 100 lists per Space), three Whiteboards, and you get limited access to some task views, such as Gantt and Workload (100 uses).</p>
<p><strong>The cheapest paid </strong><strong>plan </strong><strong>starts at <span class="pricing-link"><span data-currency="USD" class="currency-symbol ">$</span><span data-currency="USD" data-price="7.00" class="currency-value">7.00</span></span></strong><strong>per month (per member), and it </strong><strong>provides</strong><strong> unlimited storage,</strong> plus access to integrations like Google Drive, Dropbox, and Box. You can add five guests (plus two more with each additional member), set user permissions, and schedule 1,000 automations per month, which is a solid offer. You can also add unlimited view-only guest seats.</p>
<p><strong>ClickUps Business plan offers the best value</strong><strong>,</strong><strong> and it will likely be enough for any type of business</strong>. It includes full access to all task views, 10,000 automations per month, as well as advanced reports and the ability to add 10 guests, with an additional five per member.</p>
<p>ClickUp has no shortage of tools, which makes it a worthy competitor to big players like monday.com and Smartsheet. But unlike these platforms, <strong>ClickUp makes most of its premium features available even on its lower-tiered plans, giving you great value at an affordable price</strong>.</p>

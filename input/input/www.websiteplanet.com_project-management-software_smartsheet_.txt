<p>After spending an unreasonable number of days playing with charts and spreadsheets, <strong>I can say that it’s an outstanding software for managing timelines, creating reports, and organizing teams of all sizes.</strong> And though it never gets prettier, it does get a lot easier. Eventually.</p>
<p>Smartsheet offers a 30-day free trial and is available in English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, and Japanese.</p>
<p>But the only thing that really matters is whether Smartsheet is right for you and your project. That’s what we’re here to find out.</p>

<h3>Let Workflows Do the Hard Work for You</h3>
<p>Workflows are automatic processes that get triggered depending on the value of a cell. Which sounds a lot like an average spreadsheet function, and they can be. But <strong>the difference is that workflows aren’t limited to a single document.</strong></p>
<p>For example, in D&amp;D, spells are either ranged or centered on the player. To make my life easier, I simply set the range of self-centered spells to “0” and used a workflow to write “Self” on all spell sheets with a range of 0.</p>
<p>You can create workflows that send an alert to someone on your team (either on Smartsheet or by Teams or a Slack channel), request approvals on changes, or automatically send forms. <a href="https://www.websiteplanet.com/goto.php?id=2445548&lang=en&deep_id=0&deep_name=&btn_name=VendorExternalUrl_2445548_vendor_2445548_594975&post_id=2445548&pageview_id=0&direct_link=&type=vendor&amp=0"  rel="sponsored noopener norefferer" data-id="2445548" class="clickoutLink boldLink shortC" target="_blank" onclick="trackClickout('event', 'clickout', 'VendorExternalUrl_2445548_vendor_2445548_594975', 'smartsheet', this,  true  );"><strong>The workflow interface lets you </strong><strong>do all of this visually</strong></a><strong>,</strong> using diagrams instead of having to type complicated functions.</p>
<p><strong>Not only do workflows save everyone time, they reduce human error.</strong> Even if there’s a mistake in the workflow itself, you can correct the logic by changing or deleting the faulty steps in the process, without having to go case-by-case.</p>

<h3>Team Dashboard &amp; Communication</h3>

<h3>Support Broke My Heart, Then Helped Me Heal It</h3>
<p>Smartsheet offers three options for customer support. You can call them directly, email them, or use their ticket system. Or so they say. In practice,<strong> unless you have a plan with pro-support, there’s only one way to contact customer support.</strong></p>

<li>How do I set up a project for a freelancer who’s only temporarily on board?</li>
<li>How can I change access privileges for different team members?</li>
<li>Can I use the data from one column as the values in the dropdown selection for another?</li>

He asked me pointed questions about my needs and took the time to solve them step by step. He even offered to schedule another call with me if I needed it.</p>

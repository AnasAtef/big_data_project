<h3><div id="support">Live Support Difficult to Contact</div></h3>
<p><strong>I didn&#8217;t have the greatest experience with Northwest Registered Agent&#8217;s customer support.</strong> Even though it offers support via phone, online chat, and email, I was only able to contact support successfully via email.</p>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter"><span class="ll-smallimg-wrapper " style="max-width: 376px"><span class="ll-img-wrapper "  style="padding:63.829787234043% 0 0; max-width: 850px;"><noscript><img src="https://dt2sdf0db8zob.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/northwest-registered-agent-review-5.png"  width="376" height="240" alt="Chat support window with other support options stated" /></noscript><img data-src="https://dt2sdf0db8zob.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/northwest-registered-agent-review-5.png"   width="376" height="240" alt="Chat support window with other support options stated" class="border ll-img"/></span></span><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Northwest Registered Agent&#8217;s online chat was never online to chat with</figcaption></figure>
<p><strong>Northwest Registered Agent advertises that their online chat is available from 6:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. PST</strong> but I was never able to find the service online. The message I received every time I tried to contact them was that I should contact email or phone support instead.</p>
<p>The same goes for the phone support. <strong>I called several times throughout </strong><strong>an entire</strong><strong> week with no answer.</strong> I was asked to leave a message, contact support online, or via email. I eventually chose to make an attempt using email.</p>
<figure class="wp-caption aligncenter"><span class="ll-img-wrapper "  style="padding:41.643582640813% 0 0; max-width: 850px;"><noscript><img src="https://dt2sdf0db8zob.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/northwest-registered-agent-review-6.png"  width="1083" height="451" alt="email support message" /></noscript><img data-src="https://dt2sdf0db8zob.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/northwest-registered-agent-review-6.png"   width="1083" height="451" alt="email support message" class="border ll-img"/></span><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Northwest Registered Agent customer support does exist. I was beginning to wonder</figcaption></figure>
<p>Success at last! I was finally able to receive an answer. It took about 2.5 hours to receive a reply, which isn&#8217;t too long for email support. I was also happy to learn from the reply that <strong>Northwest Registered Agent offers something a lot of other business formation services don&#8217;t: Spanish-speaking support.</strong></p>
<p>As for online support and reference materials, Northwest Registered Agent&#8217;s aren&#8217;t stellar, but they’re more than adequate. <strong>It has</strong><strong> an impressive library of free forms and form templates.</strong> There are lots of links to other pages both within and off the website, but no video or other media. Compared to some of the other companies we&#8217;ve reviewed, there&#8217;s definite room for improvement.</p>

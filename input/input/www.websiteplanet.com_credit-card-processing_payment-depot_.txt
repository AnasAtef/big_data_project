<h3 class="shortcode-faq-question-inline">Are Payment Depot’s subscription plans worth it?</h3>
<span class="shortcode-faq-answer-inline">For most businesses, I’d say so. You could <a href="#pricing" onclick="trackClickout('event', 'clickin', 'anchorLink', 'pricing', this,  );">save up to 40% with its subscription pricing</a> over more traditional alternatives if you have a high monthly card volume. On the other hand, small businesses with a lower monthly income will benefit more from its affordable interchange+ pricing.</span> 
<h3 class="shortcode-faq-question-inline">What percentage does Payment Depot take?</h3>
<span class="shortcode-faq-answer-inline">Payment Depot does not charge percentage-based markups on transactions on its subscription plans. Instead, you’ll be paying a fixed monthly subscription fee priced according to the amount of money you process each year. On the other hand, Payment Depot’s interchange+ pricing comes with <span class="pricing-link">0.2%-1.95%</span> markup on transactions, but you won’t pay any monthly fee.</span> 
<h3 class="shortcode-faq-question-inline">What payment methods does Payment Depot process?</h3>
<span class="shortcode-faq-answer-inline">Payment Depot can process all major credit and debit cards. It also supports ACH transfers.
